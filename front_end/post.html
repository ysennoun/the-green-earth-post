Can agroecology feed the world ?

November 20, 2022 posted by Shireen Oum

What is agroecology ?

Agroecology is a way of designing production systems that rely on the functionalities offered by ecosystems.

Is agroecology a permanent solution?

According to several scientific studies, agroecology can feed the world. Indeed, this is possible if we reduce waste and especially if we reduce our meat consumption. It would be enough for a third of the cereals produced to feed animals to be “reallocated” for human food in order to compensate for the drop in world imports.

Preserving agricultural land

In addition, it must be associated with the practice of fallowing, which consists of keeping agricultural land unused for a certain period to allow it to replenish its water reserves and its production capacity. Indeed, it is the preservation of biodiversity, the improvement of water quality, the fight against erosion, soil restoration, carbon sequestration are at stake. A good quality of land allows a better yield of cereal production.
